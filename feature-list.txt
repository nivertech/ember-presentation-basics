* The app comprises a header.
	* title        - The caption "Ember Mail" is on the left.
	* user 		   - The user name is on the right.
	* emails-count - Emails count, and unread emails count

* There are two pages
	* Page "emails"
		* emails 		  - Shows the list of emails (email-summary)
		* noEmails        - When there's no emails, show "Sorry, nobody loves you"
		* highlight   	  - Unread emails are highlighted.
		* previewed-email - Clicking on an email shows previews it, and marks as read
	* Page "about"
